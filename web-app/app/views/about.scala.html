Welcome to the cycling site!

The intent of the site is to provide some simple metrics for cycling (or other kind of sporting) activities, trail descriptions, biking videos.

The metrics page compares yearly aggregated data, such as distance, hours ridden, elevation gained. The yearly data is also presented as a heatmap, so it is easy to compare the efforts for a given period of the year. Currently I'm using Strava and Garmin Connect to track my activities, so the data feed is collected via the Strava API. Let me know if you are interested to see other kind of metrics useful to analyze the riding data.

Stay connected!

I'd love to hear your feedback! If you have any questions, complains or even compliments feel free to contact me. Thank you for visiting and I'm waiting you to come back for new content.
